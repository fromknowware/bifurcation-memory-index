What am I looking at?
The Ramification Index
The RI is a macroeconomic indicator built on DRAM memory prices. DRAM sits at the intersection of corporate investment and consumer demand — making it one of the earliest leading signals in the global economy. The current reading is 0.863, the highest since 1995.
Why DRAM?
6 of 6 NBER recessions since 1980 were preceded by a Ramification Index downturn. Prices swing 50–80% within a single year. Only three companies — Samsung, SK Hynix, and Micron — control ~95% of global supply, making the market unusually sensitive to macro shifts.
The 2026 Bifurcation
The v2 index splits into two sub-indices: RC (commodity DRAM, +293% YoY) and RAI (HBM for AI accelerators, +18% YoY). When they diverge past 1.0, the index bifurcates — a structural event documented as RAMageddon 2026. Current gap: 1.20.
